About Us

Company History

Our company is an retailer of Porsche, Bentley, Lamborghini, and Bugatti, and many more vehicles conveniently located in New York City respected as one of the most successful luxury and performance automotive dealers in the world. We have unmatched service continuously from 1995-Present.

Our success is reflected in the professionalism and dedication to our clients and the lifestyle for which we have become known. Our specialists demonstrate shared enthusiasm for supremely refined vehicles and can expertly navigate our clients through every option from a wide selection of quality pre-owned automobiles.
